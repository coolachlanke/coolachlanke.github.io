About

Hi there! I'm Lachlan, a recent graduate in Electrical/Electronics Engineering and Mathematics from NSW, Australia. My interests cover a wide range of STEM topics, however I am particulatly passionate about machine learning, electronics, PCB design, embedded computing and 3D modelling.

In terms of personal projects, I enjoy the art of combining 3D modelling, PCB/electronics design and software to create things I find interesting, educational, or just cool! Several of these projects are availble on my projects page, including RGB LED programmable sunglasses and a Rubik's cube solving robot!

On the weekends, I enjoy running and speedsolving Rubik's cubes and other twisty puzzles. At the moment I am training for a sub-3 hour marathon and am aiming at getting my Rubik's cube solve times under 10 seconds. You might also catch me learning German, hiking, cycling or servicing my 3D printer farm!
